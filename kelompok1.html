ZOORASSIC PARK

They're back!

Perth Zoo turns a whopping 120 this year and to celebrate the dinosaurs of Zoorassic Park are back!

Just as our living Zoo changes, these prehistoric creatures will be changing too. Use the discovery trail to record the creatures you spot on your travels. Be sure to visit regularly to see how these worlds evolve.

Australian Megafauna - a Moving Beast

We know we promised you a world-first with Australian prehistoric megafauna joining their roarsome dinosaur friends, but we’re sticklers for detail and obsessed with ensuring they’re as accurate as possible. So this means they just aren’t quite ready to get on the plane and join us.

But fear not, we have extra roaring, moving dinosaurs warming up the Zoo until the Aussies arrive later in the Zoorassic Park season. Sign-up to Zoo News for updates on when they'll be stomping in!

Playleozone

9:30am - 3:00pm

Little palaeontologists will have a dino-riffic time at our bespoke playleozone.

They can get hands on, uncovering dinosaur fossils hidden in our special digging pits, or get creative with cool dinosaur colouring-ins!

Our new friends will only be visiting for a limited time.

Zoo Friends membership

As a Zoo Friends member you can keep visiting the Zoo as many times as you like for an entire year. That means you can see the dinosaurs of Zoorassic Park again and again and again.

Member Benefits

  • Unlimited FREE entry to Perth Zoo for 12 months*
  • 10% discount at Perth Zoo’s Café’s and Zoonique Shop
  • FREE entry to associated zoos around Australia
  • Priority entry through peak periods
  • Annual subscription to News Paws, Perth Zoo’s quarterly magazine
  • Access to exclusive members’-only events
  • 10% off Perth Zoo life-sized Zoo Paw Prints and animal adoptions

*General entry only. Excludes ticketed events and special programs.
